The History of Computers

Embark on a journey into the realm of computers with the South Hills mobile app. The app features a captivating video series offering insights into the history, evolution, and intricacies of computers. In the first video, "The History of Computers," discover the early inventions and influential figures shaping the modern world. The second video, "Generations of Computers," explores the technological evolution across five generations, from vacuum tube-based machines to ULSI microprocessor-based marvels. Understand the unique characteristics and limitations of computers in this enlightening exploration. In the third video, "Types of Computer Memory," unravel the mysteries of how computers store and process data. Gain insights into primary and secondary memory types, including RAM, ROM, and various storage devices.
